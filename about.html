About AIPLAT

Built to help people find the right AI tools — fast, curated, and without the noise.

What is AIPLAT?

AIPLAT (AI Platform) is a curated directory of the best artificial intelligence tools available today. We cut through the overwhelming noise of thousands of new AI releases to surface what actually matters — organized cleanly by category, use case, and pricing model.

We started AIPLAT because finding the right AI tool for a specific job was frustrating. Lists were outdated, recommendations were paid placements, and there was no place that combined curation quality with ease of navigation.

Our editorial team tests and evaluates tools before listing them. We only add tools that are genuinely useful, actively maintained, and worth your time. We never accept payment to list or promote tools — our rankings reflect merit, not money.
